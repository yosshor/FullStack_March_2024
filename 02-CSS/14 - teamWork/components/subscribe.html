Follow every beat of the music business

Money is changing everything in the music business. As streaming revenue grows, labels are bidding for hot acts, private equity players are buying up publishing catalogs, and online platforms are paying more than ever for the rights their businesses depend on. Billboard Pro breaks down the business, with incisive reporting, insightful analysis, and enlightening interviews with the artists and executives behind these changes.

Every number on 200+ Billboard charts

Billboard has been tuning in the Hot 100 since Elvis joined the Army – and its charts are still the industry standard. So are the Billboard 200 album chart, Hot Latin Songs, Hot Country Songs – and new charts like the Global 200 and Songwriters and Producers.

Breaking down the business behind the music

Billboard’s Deep Dive reports make sense of the industry’s fast-changing sectors – explaining how to take advantage of the vinyl boom, make sense of the growing African music business, and analyze and monetize the kind of super fans fueling the K-pop explosion.

The Billboard Bulletin industry briefing

Even in the streaming age, Billboard offers a daily download of the latest music business news and analysis – a sort of greatest hits of the website’s coverage of every sector of the industry, from law to Latin.

Who waits in line? Get VIP status at Billboard events, from Latin Music Week to the Music and Money conference. A Billboard Pro membership offers exclusive access to private events, first crack at limited-capacity presentations, and – of course – a free drink or two.
